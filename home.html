Guide
Dashboard

This is your personal dashboard which can give you an estimate of your economic performance, each statistic is based on various calculations taken from both StockX and Custom inventories.

Filter

You can select what filter to use for fetching every single stats, you can choose from:
- Month
- This Year
- Lifetime

Graph

The Graph changes each time you select a different filter, the graph counts only the profit less the purchases but it doesnt count the expenses.

Profit

Profit is the sum of sales from this period minus the sum of purchases from this period minus all expenses from this period.

Inventory Sold

Inventory sold is simply the sum of each item sold in the selected period.

Inventory Value

Inventory value is the sum of the average DeadStock price of each product from your inventory, it calculated based on the items that are still on your inventory and are not yet sold.

Purchases

It is the sum of the prices at which you bought the various items.
